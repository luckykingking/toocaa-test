Troubleshooting framework (diagnosis tree)

A good troubleshooting page is not a long article. It is a decision tree with experiments.

  • Symptom → define clearly with photos
  • Quick checks (focus, material flatness, airflow, lens cleanliness)
  • Experiments (change one variable at a time)
  • Fix (settings adjustment or hardware maintenance)
  • Escalation (ticket template + what to upload)
