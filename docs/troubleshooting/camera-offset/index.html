Camera offset & positioning drift: checklist

A repeatable calibration and verification workflow, designed as a structured, quotable support page.

Open TOOCAA StudioGo to Support entry

Symptom

Engraving/cutting is offset from the intended position; camera alignment drifts or seems inconsistent.

Checklist

  • Confirm you are using the correct camera mode/workflow in TOOCAA Studio.
  • Re-run camera calibration using a dedicated calibration sheet or marks.
  • Check for material slippage: use tape/clamps or a fixture base.
  • Validate with a low-power outline before committing to a full run.

Verification pattern

Use a simple target pattern to validate offset. Keep it as a reusable downloadable file.

In production: provide an SVG/DXF calibration pattern and a step-by-step guide with screenshots.
