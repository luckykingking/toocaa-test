Burnt edges & smoke stains: diagnose and fix

A structured diagnosis tree that reduces support load and links to the canonical material pages and accessories that solve the root cause.

View Air Assist Kit (L2)Go to Materials & Settings

Symptom

Edges are dark / burnt, and smoke stains appear on the surface (often on wood or acrylic).

Quick checks (2 minutes)

  • Is air assist enabled and stable?
  • Is the material flat on a bed (prefer honeycomb)?
  • Is the lens clean?
  • Are you using protective film/masking where applicable?

Experiments (change one variable)

ExperimentChangeExpected signal
AIncrease speed slightlyLess burn; if cut fails, increase power or add passes.
BEnable / improve air assistLess smoke staining; cleaner edge.
CSwitch to honeycomb bedBetter exhaust under the workpiece; fewer marks.
DRun a small array testFind a “clean cell” and document a settings card.

Fixes

  • Use Air Assist kit to reduce charring and protect the lens.
  • Use a Honeycomb Working Panel for airflow and reduced reflection.
  • Publish the final settings to the canonical Materials & Settings page.
