Laser Cut Jewelry Ideas

Create lightweight earrings, pendants, charms, display cards, and small jewelry collections with clean edges and repeatable batch layouts.

See project ideas Open settings calculator
Best material
Basswood, walnut, acrylic
Difficulty
Medium
Job types
Fine cut + Engrave

Jewelry content should focus on precision, scale, and finish. Small parts reveal every burn mark and every alignment mistake. TOOCAA works well when users choose thin, stable material, use air assist, design proper holes, and batch pieces with consistent spacing.

Quick answer

For wood earrings, use 1.5-3 mm basswood, walnut veneer, or thin birch. Keep holes at least 1.5 mm from the edge and seal pieces before adding hardware.

Frequently asked questions

Can a diode laser cut jewelry?

Yes, especially wood earrings, charms, pendants, and display cards. Acrylic depends on color and laser compatibility.

What thickness is best for laser cut earrings?

1.5-3 mm is best because it stays lightweight while still being strong enough for hardware holes.

How do I prevent jewelry from smelling burnt?

Use air assist, avoid over-burning, sand edges lightly, and let pieces air out before sealing.

Can I sell laser cut jewelry?

Yes. Use durable materials, safe finishes, consistent hardware, and clear product photos.
