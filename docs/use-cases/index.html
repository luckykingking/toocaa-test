Materials, Quality & Safety

What Makes a Laser Project Work

A finished laser project depends on compatible materials, clean settings, and a design that works in real life after it is scanned, worn, folded, touched, or displayed.

Safety

Laser-compatible materials protect the machine, the user, and the finished item. PVC, vinyl, and unknown plastics should stay out of the cutting area because harmful fumes can be produced.
