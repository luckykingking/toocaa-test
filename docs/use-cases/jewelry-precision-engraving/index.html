1) TL;DR

Jewelry engraving with a diode laser is all about precision and positioning. The pieces are small (often under 30mm), so you need a reliable jig, precise focus, and fine-detail settings. A diode laser works directly on wood, acrylic, leather, and anodized aluminum jewelry blanks. For bare metals (silver, gold), you need a marking compound like CerMark. The practical minimum text size is ~3mm cap height with clean sans-serif fonts. This page covers the complete workflow for pendant engraving and earring cutting, including batch jig templates.

3) HowTo: Engrave a batch of wooden pendants with personalized names

A workflow for producing 8–12 engraved pendants per cycle.

StepWhat you doWhy
1Prepare pendant blanks: 25mm round, 3mm thick basswood or walnut. Pre-drill a 2mm hole for the cord.Consistent blank size means one jig fits all pieces.
2Create a multi-pocket jig: cut 8–12 circle pockets (25.2mm — slight clearance) in 3mm MDF. Add registration marks.The jig holds all pendants in known positions for batch engraving.
3Drop pendants into pockets, aligning the drill holes consistently (use a notch in each pocket).Consistent hole orientation means text is always upright relative to the cord.
4Set focus on the pendant surface (not the jig). Enable air assist.Pendant surface is higher than the jig — focus must be set on the actual engrave surface.
5Engrave names: 45% power / 6000 mm/min / 1 pass. Use a clean sans-serif font at ≥4mm cap height.Lower power for delicate pieces; font size above 4mm ensures readability on small surfaces.
6Remove pendants, apply a light coat of Danish oil or wax, thread cords. Done!Finish enhances the engraved contrast and protects the wood.
Font tip: For jewelry, use bold sans-serif fonts (Futura, Montserrat, Arial Black). Thin scripts can look beautiful but fall apart below 5mm. Always run a font test at your target size. Array Test CSV

4) Common questions

From jewelry makers and personalization shops.

Can a diode laser engrave metal jewelry?

A diode laser cannot engrave bare metals like silver or gold. However, it can mark anodized aluminum, painted/coated metals, and stainless steel with a marking spray (CerMark/Enduramark). For wood, acrylic, and leather jewelry, it works directly.

What is the smallest readable text size?

With a diode laser, the practical minimum is about 3mm cap height using a clean sans-serif font (Arial, Futura). Below 3mm, letters blur together. Always test with your specific font and material.

How do I hold small jewelry pieces during engraving?

Use a custom nest jig: laser-cut pockets in MDF matching each piece's shape. For round pendants, cut circle pockets. Blue painter's tape on the honeycomb bed also prevents tiny pieces from shifting.

Will laser engraving damage gemstones?

Diode lasers can damage heat-sensitive stones (opals, emeralds, pearls). Always remove or mask gemstones before engraving nearby areas. Test on a sample first.
